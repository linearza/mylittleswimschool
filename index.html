About me

Hi, My name is Daniella and I'm here to help your little one feel safe and confident when swimming. It is so important for our kids to learn the skills required to be able to confidently play and have fun in the water, whether it be in the ocean, at a friend's pool, at school or wherever else.  Swimming is a skill every child needs and deserves to learn. Swimming is also an amazing way for your child to keep active and build up strength and muscle tone for everyday activities. It is also very beneficial for your child's coordination and balance.

Here at my little swim school your child is my priority.  I have an absolute passion for kids and swimming. I myself was a swimmer for many years; I retired from the sport in 2011. I went as far as to represent South Africa numerous times at FINA World Champs. I have my honours degree in Sport Science and I am also a qualified Sports Massage therapist. I'm so excited for you to get involved so I can help your little one reach his/her potential and feel safe and confident in the water!
